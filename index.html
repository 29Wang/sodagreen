-----Introduction-----

In May 2004, Sodagreen introduced their first single at the School Rock event at National Chengchi University in Taipei, marking the start of their journey in Mandarin pop music. Fast forward to November 3, 2007, they wowed audiences at the Taipei Arena with their "Unparalleled Beauty" concert, making history as the first indie band to headline at this renowned venue. Sodagreen's unique indie sound not only sets them apart but also secures them a special place in the music world.

--------Albums--------

Spring:Sunlight

"Spring:Sunlight" is an album released by Sodagreen in 2009. Most of the lyrics and music were composed solely by Wu Qingfeng. In 2010, Sodagreen was nominated for the Best Arrangement and Best Album Producer awards at the 21st Golden Melody Awards for this album, and also won the Best Music Video award.

Summer:Mania

'Summer:Mania' is the second album in Sodagreen's "Vivaldi Project", released on September 11, 2009. The name of this project is derived from Vivaldi's violin concertos 'The Four Seasons'.On January 27, 2010, the album won the Supreme Pop Music Awards for Song of the Year and Best Band.

Autumn:Stories

'Autumn: Stories' is the third album in Sodagreen's "Vivaldi Project", released in 2013. The lyrics and music were mostly written and composed by Wu Qingfeng alone. In 2013, for this album, Sodagreen won the Most Popular Group of the Year award at the 7th CRHK Music Awards and the Most Popular Album on iTunes Store in 2013.

Winter:Encore

'Winter Encore' is the final album in "Vivaldi Project", released on November 4, 2015. The album won Best Mandarin Album, Best Band, and Best Album Producer at the 27th Golden Melody Awards. The song "He Raised His Right Hand to Name" won Best Arranger and Best Lyricist at the 27th Golden Melody Awards respectively.
